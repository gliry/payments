Questions?
Answered.

Is it non-custodial?

Yes — your funds are always yours. The moment an invoice is paid, assets land directly in your smart account. You only grant OmniFlow permission to rebalance between your addresses — and your address is the same across all chains.

Is it secure?

We only use audited protocols under the hood. And you always see exactly what you're signing before confirming anything.

Do senders need to do anything differently?

No — for senders it looks like a regular transfer. They just pay your address.

What if a bridge fails?

We use CCTP v2 for all cross-chain transfers — Circle's native stablecoin protocol, one of the most reliable in the space.

How is this different from Request Finance?

Request Finance requires senders to pick the token and chain for each payment. With OmniFlow, receivers set their terms once — every incoming payment auto-converts to your preferred token. No coordination, no back-and-forth.

How do I get started?

Just connect your wallet — nothing to deploy. You get a smart account instantly, usable across all supported chains.

What does it cost?

Free during beta — no gas fees for most operations on supported networks. We don't take additional fees on top of protocol costs.

Which chains and tokens are supported?

12 chains supported. Most tokens on each chain — via LI.FI for single-chain swaps and Circle CCTP v2 for cross-chain transfers.
