FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Is OmniFlow custodial?

No. OmniFlow is fully non-custodial. Each user gets an isolated smart account (ERC-4337) that only they control. We never hold funds, private keys, or seed phrases. Your wallet signs every transaction.

Which chains are supported?

OmniFlow supports 30+ EVM chains including Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, Avalanche, and Sonic. We add new chains regularly through our LI.FI and Circle CCTP v2 integrations.

How does gasless work?

OmniFlow uses Account Abstraction (ERC-4337) with a paymaster. Gas fees are sponsored or deducted from the payment amount — recipients never need native tokens to receive funds.

Is OmniFlow audited?

We are planning a Trail of Bits audit for Q3 2026. Our smart contracts are open-source and built on audited primitives: ZeroDev Kernel v3.3, Safe modules, and Circle CCTP v2.

What tokens can I send and receive?

Any ERC-20 token on any supported chain. Senders pay in their preferred token, and recipients receive in theirs. OmniFlow handles cross-chain swaps and bridging automatically via LI.FI.

How do I integrate OmniFlow?

OmniFlow provides a REST API and dashboard. Upload a CSV of recipients, or integrate programmatically. API keys, webhooks for status updates, and batch operations — all documented at docs.omniflow.dev.

What does it cost?

OmniFlow charges a small fee per transaction. No monthly subscriptions, no minimum volumes. Free during beta — start building today and only pay when you scale.
